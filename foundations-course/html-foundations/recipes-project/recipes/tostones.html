Ingredients

4 servings

  • 2 green plantain
  • 10 tablespoons oil for frying
  • 6 cups of cold water
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Peel plantain and cut into 1-inch slices. Fill a bowl with 6 cuos of cold water.
  2. Heat oil in a large deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add plantains in an even layer and fry on both sides until golden brown, about 3-1/2 minutes per side.
  3. Transfer fried plantain slices to a cutting board and flatten them with a small plate.
  4. Dip flattened plantains in the bowl of cold water.
  5. Reheat oil in skillet over medium-high heat. Cook flattened plantains for about 1 minute on each side.
  6. Season to taste with salt and serve immeadiately.
